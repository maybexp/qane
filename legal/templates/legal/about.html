How it works


Qane.net works by letting users anonymously ask questions. Once a question is asked, a maximum of 40 answers can be given before the thread is closed. Answers with low quality may be deleted in order to prevent the thread from being spammed with bad answers.

What are the blue check marks?

The blue check marks are verified people. Comments get verified when they are made by people who fill at least 1 of the following conditions:

  • Professor, celebrity, or other people known in their community
  • The person/brand talked about in said post

Rules

We usually delete content for the following reasons:

  • For spreading misinformation
  • Illegal use of the site
  • Low quality
